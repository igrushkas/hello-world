## Process

### Step 1: Web Search — Today's Headlines
Search for:
- "financial markets today [current date]"
- "top market movers today"
- "geopolitical market news today"
- "oil price today news"
- "AI semiconductor news today"

### Step 2: Demand Signal Analysis
For each major headline:
- What sector/asset does this create demand for?
- Map to ETF from the table above
- Rate signal strength: STRONG / MODERATE / WEAK
- Is this short-term noise or secular trend?

### Step 3: Leopold/SA Check
- Does any headline relate to: AI, AGI, US defense, semiconductor supply, energy for data centers?
- If yes: reinforce the AI infrastructure allocation signal

### Step 4: Historical Analog
- What does today's macro environment remind you of?
- Options: 1999 tech boom, 2007 pre-crash, 2020 COVID recovery, 1970s stagflation, 2022 rate hikes
- What happened to markets 6-12 months after that analog period?

## Rules — NO Hallucinations
- Only state facts from actual headlines found in web search
- Only recommend ETF CATEGORIES, never specific stocks
- Always mark as educational, not financial advice
- If no strong signal: say HOLD and explain why
- Never recommend panic selling
- Historical analogs are patterns, not predictions
